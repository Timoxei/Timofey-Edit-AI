**Her narrative** (campaign site, interviews, Democracy Now 6/30/26): her father was taken by ICE when she was in 7th grade, detained ~3 years, and deported in 2008 by a "cruel immigration system" — framed as anti-Muslim War-on-Terror targeting.
**The record:** Abdelkareem Adnan Kawas (Jordanian national):
- **1995** — felony conviction, Richmond City Circuit Court (VA), making a false statement.
- **Aug 2006** — guilty plea, Bergen County NJ, third-degree **theft by deception** (inflated property values, $500–$74,999); sentenced Oct 2006 to 3 years, served ~15 months.
- Overstayed a 1989 tourist visa; **failed to appear at his 2004 immigration hearing**; ordered removed; **Third Circuit denied his petition Dec 24, 2008** — under the Bush administration, not a Trump-era action.
- **Official court record (primary):** *Kawas v. Attorney General*, No. 08-2245 (3d Cir., Dec. 24, 2008), Agency No. A79-130-693 — https://www2.ca3.uscourts.gov/opinarch/082245np.pdf — ✅ CONFIRMED (PDF text verified)
