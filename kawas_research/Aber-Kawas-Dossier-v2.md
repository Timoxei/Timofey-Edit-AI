### 7. Father's deportation narrative vs. the court record
Her narrative (on camera, [Democracy Now](https://www.democracynow.org/2026/6/30/aber_kawas)): her father was taken by ICE, held ~3 years, and deported to Jordan "the year Obama was elected," which she blames on a "cruel immigration system."

The record: her father overstayed a 1989 tourist visa, **missed his 2004 immigration hearing**, was ordered removed, and the **Third Circuit denied his final petition Dec 24, 2008** — [*Kawas v. Attorney General*, No. 08-2245 (official court PDF)](https://www2.ca3.uscourts.gov/opinarch/082245np.pdf). Reporting from state court records adds a **1995 Virginia felony** (false statement) and a **2006 New Jersey guilty plea to theft by deception**, 3-year sentence: [NY Post via AOL](https://www.aol.com/articles/father-newly-elected-aber-kawas-202738012.html). → Standard removal under the Bush administration plus a criminal record — which her "cruel immigration system" framing omits.
