### 2. Father's deportation narrative vs. the court record
Her narrative, on camera: her father was taken by ICE, detained ~3 years, and deported to Jordan "the year Obama was elected" (2008), which she attributes to a "cruel immigration system." **[8]**
The record: her father, Abdelkareem Kawas (Jordanian national), overstayed a 1989 tourist visa, **missed his 2004 immigration hearing**, was ordered removed, and the **U.S. Court of Appeals for the Third Circuit denied his final petition on December 24, 2008** (*Kawas v. Attorney General*, No. 08-2245, Agency No. A79-130-693). **[5]** Reporting based on state court records adds a **1995 Virginia felony conviction for making a false statement** and a **2006 New Jersey guilty plea to theft by deception** (inflated property values), for which he was sentenced to three years. **[6]**
→ The deportation followed standard removal proceedings under the Bush administration and a documented criminal record — which her "cruel immigration system" framing omits.
